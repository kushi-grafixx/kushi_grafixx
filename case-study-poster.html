Brutalist Elegance

Visual Communication • Poster Campaign • Typography

Client

Architexture Gallery

Timeline

2 Weeks

Role

Visual Designer

Designed for an upcoming architectural exhibition in Berlin, the brief called for a poster campaign that felt raw yet highly disciplined. The challenge was merging brutalist concrete textures with sharp, legible Swiss-style typography.

We used striking red accents against a monochromatic foundation to guide the viewer's eye. The resulting posters became a massive hit, acting not just as informational flyers, but as collectible art pieces for attendees.
