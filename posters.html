Lumi Wellness: Poster Series

Designing for print requires a different kind of discipline—one where negative space carries as much weight as the content itself.

For Lumi Wellness, we created a series of posters that prioritize high-impact minimalism. The goal was to communicate a sense of refined tranquility. We focused on a limited color palette, premium tactile textures (simulated through high-resolution dither patterns), and balanced typography that leads the eye across the page without feeling hurried.

This series demonstrates that you don't need a lot of noise to make a statement. In a world of digital clutter, a well-balanced, minimalist poster is a breath of fresh air—a quiet but powerful visual pause.
